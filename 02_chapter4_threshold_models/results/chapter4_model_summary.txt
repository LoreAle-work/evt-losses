Main interpretation:
The threshold model estimates the tail behavior of daily SPY losses
using exceedances above high thresholds. The key parameter is xi.
Positive xi estimates suggest heavy-tailed behavior in extreme losses.
The Chapter 4 GPD results are broadly consistent with the Chapter 3 GEV results.
