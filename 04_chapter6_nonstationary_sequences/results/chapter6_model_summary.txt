Interpretation guide:
If the volatility-dependent scale model improves AIC/BIC or likelihood,
then there is evidence that exceedance severity changes with volatility.
If beta_vol is positive, fitted GPD scale increases in high-volatility periods.
Positive xi estimates continue to support heavy-tailed losses.
The 90th and 95th percentile volatility states are crisis scenarios.
